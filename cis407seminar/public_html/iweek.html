I-Week and Night Is Coming!

International Week (I-Week) is quickly approaching. This years' theme is Let's Go Somewhere! Pack your bags and get ready to join ISA on a trip around the world starting April 27th and capped off May 2nd with our annual closing event I-Night which will feature lots of delicious food and fantastic performances from the international community here on the University of Oregon campus.
