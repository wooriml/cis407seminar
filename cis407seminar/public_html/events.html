Coffee Hour

ISA's longest running event. Every Friday ISA hosts a Coffee Hour where students of all different nationalities gather to socialize, eat food and drink Coffee.

Coffee Hour Winter 2017:

Coffee Hour is moving to a new location so that we can accommodate more people, more food and more fun! Coffee Hour will be taking place this Spring at the Global Scholars Hall Great Room 123.

Coffee hour will be held on every Friday besides weeks 5 and 7. It will still run from 4-6 pm. We hope to see you there!
