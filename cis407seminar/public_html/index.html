We Are ISA

We are the International Student Association at the University of Oregon. We are an international student group whose mission is to support and help foster the international community on the UO campus.

Coffee Hour is Changing Locations (Again)

Starting Winter 2017 Coffee Hour will be getting bigger and more exciting! On every Friday besides weeks 5 and 7, we will be having Coffee Hour in Global Scholars Hall Room 123 aka the great room from 4-6. Come catch some rays and eat some delicious food with ISA! Learn More
